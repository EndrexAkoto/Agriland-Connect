Our Mission and Vision

Empowering landowners and urban farmers to work together for sustainable food production and community growth.

Promote Local Food Production

We are committed to promoting local food production by connecting landowners with urban farmers to make the best use of underutilized land for sustainable agriculture.

Community Sustainability

We believe in fostering community sustainability through the utilization of underutilized land for local food production, promoting a healthier and more self-sufficient community.

Partnership and Collaboration

We are dedicated to building partnerships and collaborations between landowners and urban farmers, creating a network of support for sustainable agriculture and community development.

Our Services

Discover the services we offer to landowners and urban farmers to facilitate the leasing of underutilized land for sustainable food production.

Land Leasing

We assist landowners in leasing their underutilized land to urban farmers, ensuring a smooth and mutually beneficial agreement for sustainable agriculture.

Urban Farming Partnership

We facilitate partnerships between landowners and urban farmers, creating opportunities for collaboration and growth in sustainable food production.

Community Engagement

We promote community engagement by connecting landowners and urban farmers, fostering a sense of shared responsibility for local food production and sustainability.

Why Choose Agriland Connect?

  • Utilize Unused Land: Transform vacant lots and underutilized spaces into productive urban farms.
  • Build Community: Foster connections between landowners, farmers, and local food enthusiasts.
  • Promote Sustainability: Reduce food miles and increase access to fresh, locally-grown produce.
  • Generate Income: Create new revenue streams for landowners and opportunities for urban farmers.
